The learner asked to generate all lessons + hands-on sessions as HTML at once, rather than the
teach-method default of one lesson per session sized to the current ZPD.

Why it matters for future sessions:
- The later lessons (5 & 6) were written without evidence of what the learner mastered in 1–4, so their
  decompositions of EnM #3/#5/#1 are framed as *sketches to critique*, not settled answers. Treat them as
  revisable when the learner returns with questions or completed exercises.
- No new evidence of mastery yet — coverage is not learning. The first real signal will be when the learner
  fills the Lesson 6 template for a row (#4/#6/#7) and brings it back. Grade that, then update the ZPD.
- Three runnable, verified code artifacts now exist (agent.py, forecast_tool.py, orchestrator.py); future
  hands-on work should build on these rather than start fresh.
