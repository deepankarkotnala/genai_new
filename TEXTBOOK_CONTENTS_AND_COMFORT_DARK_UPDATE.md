# Textbook Contents and Comfort Dark Mode Update

## What changed

- Reduced the desktop type, control, card, and spacing scale to feel closer to the previous 80% browser-zoom reference while keeping mobile touch sizing unchanged.
- Widened the main reading canvas and kept the secondary rail compact.
- Reworked the left navigation into a denser textbook-style contents list:
  - chapter numbers align in a dedicated column;
  - track labels read as book parts;
  - long titles stay on one line with ellipsis;
  - group blurbs are hidden on desktop;
  - search now says **Find chapter or topic**.
- Rebuilt the right chapter rail as a searchable textbook contents panel:
  - numbered topics;
  - shorter display labels while retaining the complete title as a tooltip;
  - one-line rows for faster scanning;
  - in-panel scrolling and active-section tracking;
  - a chapter-top shortcut.
- Softened dark mode with blue-grey surfaces, restrained lavender accents, subtler borders and shadows, and warm off-white text instead of bright white.
- Preserved the existing mobile drawer behavior and sizing.
